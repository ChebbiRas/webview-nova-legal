Privacy Policy

Split Browser

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Split Browser is designed with privacy in mind. The app stores all your data locally on your device, including:

  • Website URLs and configurations
  • Custom layouts and settings
  • Theme preferences (dark/light mode)
  • Recent browsing history

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers. Your data remains entirely under your control.

3. Third-Party Websites

When you browse websites through Split Browser, those websites may have their own privacy policies and may collect data according to their terms. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of websites you visit. Split Browser does not access or monitor the content of websites you browse.

4. Permissions

The app requires internet access to load websites. No other device permissions are required or requested. Split Browser does not access your:

  • Camera or microphone
  • Contacts or calendar
  • Location or GPS
  • Files or media storage

5. Advertising

Split Browser may display advertisements through Google AdMob to support app development. AdMob may collect and use data according to Google's privacy policy. For more information about how Google collects and uses data, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Privacy Information

You can learn about ad personalization and opt-out options at Google Ad Settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Split Browser does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. The app is designed for general audiences and does not contain age-restricted features.

7. Data Security

While all data is stored locally on your device, we recommend:

  • Using device security features (lock screen, password protection)
  • Keeping your device's operating system updated
  • Being cautious when browsing untrusted websites

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. Changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

9. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • You have full control over your data
  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling the app
  • You can clear specific data using the app's settings
